WE ARE WISU YAOUNDE

We are the Wimbum Students’ of Nkambe and Ndu Sub Divisions of Donga - Mantung Division, -Conscious of our being and in cognizance of our obligations to our people and Fatherland -With unwavering faith in our elders’ philosophical convention that, Unity is strength, Abih Yu Ngir,. Here by resolve to form a Union. This union shall be apolitical, non-profit making... We the Wimbum students’ union -WISU, of the Donga-Mantung Division of the Northwest Region of Cameroon have been existing for 40 years now. We gather pupils, students and youths of all works of life each year for our annual conventions. We have more than 20,000 members both within and without the country and branches in all the 10 Regions of Cameroon as well as branches in the Diaspora. .

THE FOUNDE OF WISU

Since the inception of WISU in august 1979, our prime objective was to make maximum sensitization about the importance of education of the Mbum child. Since then, we have been rotating within the 33 villages each year, teaching, sensitizing and orientating students, pupils and other learners of the importance of education and professional training. We equally give them support and encouragement to distinguish students pupils and schools each year. We also support and sponsor some of the less privilege and orphans in our society by giving some scholarships in the form of school fees, didactic materials, writing materials, generators to schools in suburb areas as well as drinking bucket to schools to step up sanitary conditions.

How We Are

THE TIKARI

THE NFUH AND SAMBA SOCIETY

The Nfuh group was/is being headed by Ta Nformi Nfuh consisted of healthy men who were responsible for the security of the land. This was being witnessed during the migration period when these men fought and conquer lands for settlements and during war periods when there fought to protect their people. They have weapons such as guns, cutlasses, and spears

THE NWARONG

The Nwarong society is headed by Ta-Shey Nwarong. This was/is the police of the Palace. This group further has sub groups such as; Nkoh, Ma Boh, Walikang and Chumboh. They guard against stealing, witch crafts, cheating and all types of assaults. Not only to for administration they also enjoy with people in times of occasions and cry together with people in times of death or sorrow.

THE NGIRI

This group is being headed by Ta Shey Ngiri. This was/is an assembly of princes of the palace. This is group is called the gentle man’s club. This is the most peaceful of all. It has sub groups such as the Wan maboh, gwarnganjo and as well chumboh Not only to for administration they also enjoy with people in times of occasions and cry together with people in times of death or sorrow.
